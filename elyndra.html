Welcome, Truthseekers

-----------------------------------------------------------------

Tell me, seeker… What is it you desire in this realm?

Is it wealth? Power? Fame?

Or...

Perhaps something far more elusive?

Do you seek... the Truth?


You wish to face the Realmkeeper.

But know this: I cannot give you the answer you seek...for that knowledge lies beyond even my reach. I am but a Witness, bound to this sacred place, eternal and unseen. I have watched countless Seekers tread these grounds, each burning with ambition, with hope, with desperation.

This is the Realm of Elyndra...a land suspended between time and silence, where the echoes of forgotten truths still whisper through the very air. Here, only those who endure the trials may approach the threshold of understanding. To confront the Realmkeeper is to confront oneself… and not all are ready for such a reckoning.

Many have come before you.

Many have fallen.

Their names are dust now, their purpose shattered, their remains scattered across the corners of this realm like faded dreams.


But you...

You are different.

I see it in your eyes.

A fire burns in your eyes.

a passion to uncover The Truth





-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Before you, eight glowing gateways shimmer into existence, born of light and air.

These gateways each hold a Key...artifacts that will aid you in the trials ahead.













-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Suddenly, a ninth gateway emerges, larger and more imposing than the rest, towering above them like a sentinel.

When you have claimed all eight Keys, step through the ninth gate. There, you will face The Guardian . Only then shall your path to the Realmkeeper be revealed.
